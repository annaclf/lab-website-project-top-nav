    Bread

👩‍🍳 My favorite bread 👩‍🍳

Ingredients

  • 25g fresh yeast
  • 0.4 l water
  • 1 l flour
  • 1 tea spoon salt
  • 4 spoons of olive oil

Steps

  1. Melt the yeast in 37 deg water
  2. Mix in the salt and the oil
  3. Stir while adding flour
  4. Leave to rest 30 min
  5. Make your bread
  6. Leave to rest 30 min
  7. Bake ca 10 min
